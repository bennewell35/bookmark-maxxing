# Prompt: Write a Bookmark Maxxing Article

Write a Bookmark Maxxing article from the source map.

Style:

- Lead with a concrete transformation.
- Make the opening frame obvious: bookmarks become skills.
- Use short paragraphs.
- Avoid generic AI-news language.
- Attribute people and sources.
- Every major theme must produce a skill.
- End by returning to the Bookmark Maxxing series frame.

Structure:

```text
# Bookmark Maxxing #[N]: [Transformation Title]

Most people collect bookmarks.
Builders should be collecting skills.

[What was analyzed.]
[What changed.]
[The core question.]

## What My Bookmarks Say About [Topic] This Week

## Theme: [Theme]
Skill: [Skill]

## Theme: [Theme]
Skill: [Skill]

## The Bookmark Maxxing Takeaway

## How to Use This

## Sources

Closing:
Bookmarks are the input. Skills are the output.
```
